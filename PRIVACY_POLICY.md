## Overview
TinyBudget is a baby expense tracking app designed for parents. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
TinyBudget does **NOT** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All expense data, child information, and preferences are stored **locally on your device only**.

## Data Storage
- All data is stored in a local database on your device
- No data is sent to any server or third party
- No account or login is required
- No analytics or tracking tools are used

## Photos
- Receipt photos you attach are stored locally on your device
- They are never uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to export/share them

## Ads
- The app may display ads via Google AdMob
- Google AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- Premium users see no ads

## Data Backup
- The backup feature exports your database file locally
- You choose where to save or share it
- We have no access to your backups

## Children's Privacy
- The app stores children's names locally for expense categorization only
- No children's data is collected or transmitted
